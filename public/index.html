Accomplishment Steps

    Visualize your future which can be new exciting or terrible to think about.

    Think and plan creatively, probably research on what type of activities or steps u must do to achieve it.

    Think about the past obstacles that you have faced while accomplishing your dreams and how you have overcome it .Based on which you can you can overcome your future obstacles for your dreams.

    Use your support system for asking help or seeking that takes a great deal of vulnerability.

    Manage your time by making a to -do-list which allows you to potentially set time limits on reoccurring, menial tasks and also help you find gaps in your schedule to work your dream.

Finally, as you’re going through this journey to achieve your dream, think about what sparked that dream to begin with. That is think why and remeber your why for choosing it
